Spreading Joy & Down Syndrome Awareness

Project HAPPIE (Help All Preschoolers Play in Inclusive Environments) provides preschools with Down syndrome-themed books, dolls, and toys to create a more inclusive environment for all children. We’re starting in Northeast Missouri, but we believe the sky’s the limit!
